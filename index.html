How to play

The object of Tic Tac Toe is to get three in a row. You play on a three by three game board. Player is known as X and the computer is O. Players alternate placing Xs and Os on the game board until either oppent has three in a row or all nine squares are filled. X always goes first, and in the event that no one has three in a row, the stalemate is called a cat game.
